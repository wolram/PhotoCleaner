SnapSieve Privacy Policy

Our Commitment: SnapSieve is designed with privacy as a core principle. All photo analysis and processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data.

Data Collection

SnapSieve does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user data is collected, transmitted, or stored externally
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No third-party services receive your data
  • No account creation or login is required
  • No internet connection is required for the app to function

All processing, including duplicate detection, similarity analysis, and quality assessment, is performed 100% locally on your Mac using Apple's built-in frameworks.

Photo Access

SnapSieve accesses your photos exclusively through Apple's PhotoKit framework with your explicit permission. Here is how we handle your photos:

  • Read Access: We request read access to analyze your photo library for duplicates, similar photos, and quality assessment
  • Delete Permission: We request permission to delete photos only when you explicitly confirm deletion of selected items
  • Local Processing: All image analysis, including perceptual hashing, feature extraction, and quality scoring, happens entirely on your device
  • No Uploads: Your photos are never uploaded, copied, or transmitted outside your device

You can revoke photo library access at any time through System Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos.

Data Security

Your data security is ensured through the following measures:

  • On-Device Only: All data remains on your Mac and is never transmitted over the internet
  • Apple Sandbox: SnapSieve runs within Apple's app sandbox, limiting its access to only the resources you explicitly grant
  • No External Storage: Analysis results and app data are stored locally using Apple's secure frameworks (SwiftData)
  • No Cloud Sync: SnapSieve does not sync data to iCloud or any other cloud service

Third-Party Services

SnapSieve does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No external APIs or web services

Children's Privacy

SnapSieve does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Since no data is collected, there are no special provisions needed for children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
